1Getting Started

Creating an Account

  1. Go to the vocaBoost website.
  2. Click the Sign Up button.
  3. Enter your Full Name, email, and password.
  4. Select Student for your Role.
  5. Select your Graduation Year and Month.
  6. Click Create Account to complete registration.
Quick Sign Up

Click Continue with Google to quickly sign up with your Google account.

Logging In

  1. Go to the vocaBoost website.
  2. Click the Log In button.
  3. Enter the email and password you used to sign up.
  4. Click Continue to go to your dashboard.

Joining a Class

  1. Get the join code (6 characters) from your teacher.
  2. Find the join code input field on your dashboard.
  3. Enter the join code.
  4. Click Join Class to join the class.
Join Code Format

The join code is a 6-character alphanumeric code like ABC123. Make sure to enter it exactly as given.

2Understanding the Dashboard

After logging in, the first screen you see is the Dashboard. This is where you start all your learning.

Dashboard Components

Item Description
My Classes List of all classes you've joined
Assigned Lists Word lists assigned to each class
Progress Learning progress for each word list
Streak Number of consecutive days you've studied

Progress Indicator (Mastery Squares)

The 7 small squares next to each word list show your weekly progress.

  • Green: Days you completed learning
  • Gray: Days you haven't studied yet

When all 7 squares are green, you've completed a week of consistent learning!

Benefits of Consistent Learning

Studying a little bit every day helps you remember words much longer. Keep your streak going!

Starting a Session

  1. Find the word list you want to study on your dashboard.
  2. Click the Start Session button.
  3. Study today's new words with flashcards.
  4. Take the new word test.
  5. Review previously learned words. (No review on Day 1)
  6. Take the review test.
  7. View your results and complete the session.
It's OK to Leave Mid-Session

Your progress is saved if you leave during a session. You can continue where you left off when you return. However, if you leave during a test, you'll need to restart that test.

Automatic Pace Adjustment

vocaBoost automatically adjusts your learning pace based on your performance:

  • Good scores: More new words to learn
  • Low scores: Fewer new words, more review

The system adapts to the pace that works best for you.

4Using Flashcards

Flashcards are the most effective way to learn new words.

Card Layout

Front Back
Word + Part of Speech Definition + Translation + Sample Sentence

Controls

  • Flip card: Click the card or press Space or Enter
  • Hear pronunciation: Click the speaker icon

Learning Options

During flashcard study, you can use these buttons:

  • I know this: Already know this word (removes from queue)
  • Not Sure: Not familiar yet (keeps in queue)
  • Take Test: Finish studying and proceed to the test
Effective Study Tips

1. Look at the word and try to recall the meaning
2. Flip the card to check your answer
3. Read the sample sentence to understand usage
4. Listen to the pronunciation and say it out loud

PDF Download

You can download your studied words as a PDF to print. Select Download PDF from the flashcard study screen menu.

5Test Types

Multiple Choice Test

Select the correct answer from the given options. The number of options (4-10) is set by your teacher.

  1. Read the definition.
  2. Click the correct word from the options.
  3. Click Submit Test when you've answered all questions.
Navigating Questions

Click the question numbers at the bottom to jump to any question. You can skip difficult questions and come back to them later.

Written Test

Type the word based on the given definition.

  1. Read the definition.
  2. Type the corresponding word in the input field.
  3. Press Enter to move to the next question.
  4. Click Submit Test when you've answered all questions.
AI Grading

Written tests are graded by AI. Minor typos may be accepted, but it's best to type accurately.

Passing Criteria

You must score at or above the threshold set by your teacher (usually 95%) to pass.

  • Pass: Proceed to the next stage
  • Fail: Click Retake to try again

Challenge (Dispute)

If you believe the AI graded your written answer incorrectly, you can file a challenge.

  1. Find the question you want to dispute in your test results.
  2. Click the Challenge button.
  3. One challenge token is used. (You have 5 total)
  4. If your teacher approves, your score is adjusted.
Challenge Tokens

You start with 5 challenge tokens that recharge over time. Use them wisely.

6Progress & Statistics

Understanding Word Status

Each word is displayed differently based on its learning status:

Status Meaning
NEW Word learned today for the first time
PASSED Word answered correctly on test
FAILED Word answered incorrectly (needs review)
MASTERED Fully learned word (removed from review pool)

Gradebook

  1. Click Gradebook in the top menu.
  2. View all your test records.
  3. Click on any test to see detailed results.

Blind Spots

Blind Spots are words that need attention:

  • Never Tested: Words you haven't been tested on yet
  • Stale: Words not reviewed for 21+ days

After completing a session, if Blind Spots are shown, click Start Blind Spot Test to test them.

Check Mastered Words Too

Even mastered words become "Stale" after 21 days. Use the Blind Spot check to maintain your memory.

Resetting Learning Progress

Use this when you want to start a word list from the beginning.

  1. Find the Reset Progress section in Settings.
  2. Select the class and word list.
  3. Click the Reset Progress button.
  4. Type RESET to confirm.
  5. Click the confirm button to reset.
Warning: Reset Cannot Be Undone

Resetting progress deletes all learning records, test scores, and word mastery. You'll start from Day 1.

8FAQ

Q. Do I have to complete all stages every day?
No. You can leave and continue later at any time. However, if you leave during a test, you'll need to restart that test.
Q. There are too many new words. It's overwhelming.
vocaBoost automatically adjusts based on your performance. If your scores are low, new words decrease and review increases. You can also ask your teacher to adjust the learning pace.
Q. It's hard to get above 95% on tests.
The passing threshold is set by your teacher. Studying thoroughly with flashcards before taking tests will help you get better scores.
Q. Do mastered words ever appear again?
They don't appear in regular reviews, but after 21 days they become "Stale" and show up in Blind Spots. You can test them in the Blind Spot check.
Q. I entered the wrong join code.
Double-check the uppercase/lowercase letters and numbers. If it still doesn't work, ask your teacher to confirm the code.
Q. Can I study on different devices?
Yes. Log in with the same account and you can study anywhere. Works on computers, tablets, and smartphones.
Q. I forgot my password.
Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen and enter your email. A password reset link will be sent to you.
Q. What happens if I reset my learning progress?
All learning records for that word list are deleted and you start from Day 1. Previous test scores are also removed, so decide carefully.
Q. I'm running out of challenge tokens.
Challenge tokens automatically recharge over time. Only use challenges when you're confident your answer was correct.
Q. Can I use vocaBoost offline?
No, vocaBoost requires an internet connection. Your progress and test results are saved in real-time.
